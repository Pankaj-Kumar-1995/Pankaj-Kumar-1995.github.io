Coles SAP S/4HANA Cloud ERP

Business Case & Transformation Strategy

Executive Summary: Coles will transform enterprise systems by migrating from legacy ERP to SAP S/4HANA Cloud, improving agility, reducing costs, and enabling data-driven decision making.

Business Problem

Coles' legacy ERP systems (900+ interfaces, $30M annual maintenance) are increasingly unable to support business growth and digital transformation initiatives required to compete in modern retail.

Proposed Solution

Migrate to SAP S/4HANA Cloud with phased implementation across 8 business units over 36 months.

Item Amount

Key Benefits

  • 60% reduction in IT maintenance costs
  • 45% improvement in supply chain efficiency
  • Real-time financial reporting and analytics
  • Improved customer experience through better insights
  • Foundation for AI/ML and advanced analytics

Key Risks

  • Business disruption during migration (Medium Risk - 18-month mitigation plan)
  • Data migration complexity (High Risk - 200 legacy systems to integrate)
  • Change management and training (Medium Risk - $5M training budget)
  • Vendor lock-in (Low Risk - cloud-first strategy)

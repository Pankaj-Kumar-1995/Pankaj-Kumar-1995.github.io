RMIT AI Chatbot for WIL Team

Business Requirements Document

Project Goal: Implement an AI-powered chatbot using Microsoft Copilot to automate student support for RMIT's Work Integrated Learning program.

Current State Problems

  • 70% of support staff time spent on repetitive inquiries
  • Average response time: 24 hours
  • High staff burnout due to repetitive tasks
  • Limited 24/7 support availability

Target State

  • 70% reduction in manual ticket processing
  • Average response time: 2 hours for automated queries
  • 24/7 student support availability
  • Improved staff job satisfaction

Benefit Metric Target
Cost Savings Annual labor cost reduction $150,000
Student Satisfaction NPS Score improvement +25 points
Response Time Average response time 2 hours
Staff Productivity Time freed for strategic work 60% per staff member

  • Transparent escalation to human support

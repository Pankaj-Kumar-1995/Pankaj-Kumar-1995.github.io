Executive Summary

This BRD outlines the business requirements for building a machine learning-based demand forecasting system for NYC311 services.

Business Objectives

  • Reduce manual support ticket processing by 60%
  • Improve response time accuracy and resource allocation
  • Enable data-driven decision making for operational planning
  • Decrease operational costs while improving service quality

Stakeholders

  • NYC Department of Information Technology & Telecommunications
  • NYC311 Operations Team
  • Resource Planning Department
  • Data Science Team

Business Problem

NYC311 receives over 30 million calls annually but lacks accurate demand forecasting capabilities, leading to inefficient resource allocation and poor service levels during peak periods.

Key Requirements

  • Real-time demand prediction by service category
  • Location-based demand clustering and analysis
  • Seasonal and trend analysis capabilities
  • Interactive dashboard with filtering options
  • Automated alerts for demand anomalies
